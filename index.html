About Us

Johns Creek High School's Aviation Club


The Johns Creek High School Aviation Club is a student organization dedicated to exploring the science, technology, and careers behind flight. Members learn about aircraft systems, aviation history, air traffic control, and industry regulations through discussions, activities, and interactive challenges. The club provides a structured environment for students interested in flying, aerospace, or aviation-related careers to expand their knowledge and connect with peers who share similar goals!

Flights

Flexible and Structured


Take to the Skies

An introductory flight is a perfect way to get your feet off the ground and experience what its really like to fly an airplane! Flights are taken in one of our four-seat Piper Cherokee or Cessna Skyhawk Aircraft and depart from the Dekalb- Peachtree Airport in Atlanta. Your FAA Certified Flight Instructor will help customize the experience to your interests.
